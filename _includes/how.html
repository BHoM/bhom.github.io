An inclusive computational environment

Let's speak the same language

By integrating a number of concepts across existing computational tools and languages - a new means of fluid communication and collaboration is possible. With familiar terms consistently applied across platforms, the bar to access computational design is lowered. For the AEC industry, a modular structure and an open architecture built for co-creation means a platform where the professional programmer through to enthusiastic computational designer, engineer, or architect can combine efforts in one common coding ecosystem.
